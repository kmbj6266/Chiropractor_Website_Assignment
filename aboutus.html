Meghan Donohue, Massage Therapist

Meghan, is the Massage Therapist and Office Assistant Manager at St. Pete Chiropractic Clinic.  She is a Licensed Massage Therapist specializing in neuromuscular therapy, craniosacral therapy, raindrop therapy, rehabilitation and relaxation. She has been with the company since 2004. Meghan earned her general A.A in 2004 and her A.S degree from the Physical Therapy Assistant program at St. Petersburg College, St. Petersburg Florida in 2008. She specializes in orthopedic rehabilitation, pre and post surgical rehabilitation and automobile injuries. Meghan helps patients regain strength and function following surgery, car accidents, injuries, and treatment of cancer.
